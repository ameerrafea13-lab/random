Roblox Doors: Tools & Info

Roblox Doors is a popular horror game on Roblox where players explore a hotel full of monsters and puzzles. Here are some of the main tools/items you can find and use in the game:

Tool/Item Description
Flashlight Lights up dark rooms. Requires batteries to recharge.
Lighter Provides a small amount of light. Has limited fuel.
Lockpick Opens locked doors and drawers without a key.
Vitamins Temporarily increases your speed. Useful for escaping monsters.
Crucifix Can be used to repel certain entities (like Rush or Ambush) once per use.
Key Opens locked doors. Usually found in drawers or on tables.
Candle Detects entity presence by flickering and provides light.
Skeleton Key Opens special doors, such as the one to the Herb of Viridis.
Herb of Viridis Heals the player over time when held.

Tip: Always check drawers and rooms for useful items, and listen for audio cues to avoid monsters!
