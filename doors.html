Seek

Quiz

Which door numbers does Seek show up in?

Seek is a major entity in Doors. It appears in two chase sequences, usually between doors 30-40 and 70-80. When Seek spawns, the player must run through a series of rooms while obstacles block the way. Seek will chase the player and instantly kill them if it catches up. The chase ends when the player reaches a safe room and closes the door behind them.
If you don't know how to be fast in Seek, drink vitamins!

Figure

Quiz

Which door numbers does Figure show up in?

Figure is a blind entity that appears at doors 50 and 100. It relies on sound to detect players. At door 50, players must collect books and decode a code to unlock the exit, all while avoiding Figure. At door 100, Figure appears again during the final escape sequence. Crouching makes the player silent, allowing them to sneak past Figure.
If you don't know how to beat Figure, just crouch. He won't get you even if you are next to him.

Rush

Quiz

How much damage does Rush do?

Rush is a fast-moving entity that travels through rooms, flickering the lights as a warning. When Rush spawns, players must quickly hide in a closet or under a bed. If Rush finds a player in the open, it instantly kills them, dealing up to 100 damage. Rush can appear multiple times in a run.
If you don't know how to beat Rush, just hide in the closet when he flickers.

Hide

Quiz

How does Hide come?

Hide is an entity that appears if a player stays in a hiding spot (closet or bed) for too long. Hide will force the player out and deal damage. This mechanic prevents players from camping in hiding spots. Hide is triggered by being late to leave after Rush or Ambush passes.
If you don't know how to avoid Hide:
If you are hiding from Rush, get out when Rush finishes.
If you are hiding from Ambush, he comes and goes, so every time he comes and goes, get out and go back.

Ambush

Quiz

What is the main difference between Ambush and Rush?

Ambush is similar to Rush but more dangerous. Ambush will travel through the rooms multiple times, forcing players to repeatedly hide and exit hiding spots. If you stay in a hiding spot too long, Hide will force you out, so you must time your hiding carefully.
Listen for Ambush's sound and be ready to get in and out of hiding spots several times!

Screech

Quiz

How do you avoid Screech's attack?

Screech appears in dark rooms and will make a "psst" sound. If you hear it, look around quickly to find Screech. Staring at Screech will make it disappear and prevent it from attacking you.
Always look around in dark rooms if you hear "psst"!

Halt

Quiz

What should you do when Halt says "Turn Around"?

Halt is an entity that appears in a special event room. The screen will flash "Turn Around" and you must turn your character and run in the opposite direction to avoid Halt's attacks.
Always follow the "Turn Around" prompt to survive Halt!
